DATA & FILE OVERVIEW

The data is stored in one .txt file:

metaanalysis2020R2.txt

	SYMBIONT_SPECIES: name of symbiont (categorical)	
	SYMBIONT_TYPE: type of symbiont (categorical)			
	FUNCTIONAL_GROUP: functional group of symbiont (categorical)		
	FUNCTIONAL_GROUP2: other grouping of functional groups (categorical)			
	TAXONOMIC_GROUP: taxonomic group of symbiont (categorical)	
	GROUP2: other taxonomic group of symbiont (categorical)	
	FAMILY: family of symbiont (categorical)		
	GENUS: genus of symbiont (categorical)	
	HITS_Googlescholar: number of hits on google scholar for symbiont species
	Other columns give different host species (numbers refer to 1 = association, 0 = no association known)
